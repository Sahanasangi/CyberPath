👋 Welcome to Your Cyber Journey

Cybersecurity is one of the fastest-growing industries in the world. From hacking (the legal kind) to defending cloud networks, the possibilities are endless. But where do you begin?

💡 What Is a Cyber Career?

A cyber career is any role that helps protect systems, networks, data, or users from digital threats. It includes offensive roles like penetration testing, defensive roles like SOC analysts, legal experts in cyber law, and even trainers and educators.

Why Is a Career Explorer Important?

In the world of cybersecurity, there’s no single path. Some people start as developers and become ethical hackers. Others study law and protect digital rights. But many beginners feel lost — unsure of what to choose, how to start, or what skills are needed.

That’s why this Career Explorer exists — to shine a light on each path, give clarity, and help you confidently choose a role that fits **you**. Whether you're into breaking things (legally), defending networks, analyzing threats, or teaching others — your journey starts here 🛡️✨.
